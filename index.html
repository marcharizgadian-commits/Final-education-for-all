What is Quality Education?

Quality education ensures that every learner acquires the knowledge, skills, and values needed to live productive and fulfilling lives. It promotes equality, empowers communities, and supports lifelong learning.

Quality Educantion for All focuses on ensuring inclusive and equitable education for all, addressing barriers that prevent millions of learners from accessing proper learning environments.
